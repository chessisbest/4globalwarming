Global Warming Awareness

What is Global Warming?

Global warming is the long-term heating of Earth's climate system. The primary cause is the increased concentration of greenhouse gases, such as carbon dioxide (CO2) and methane (CH4), in the Earth's atmosphere.

Effects of Global Warming

Global warming leads to various environmental changes, including rising sea levels, more frequent and severe heatwaves, extreme weather events, and disruptions to ecosystems.

How Can You Help?

  • Reduce your carbon footprint by using energy-efficient appliances.
  • Use public transportation, carpool, or consider using a bike for short distances.
  • Recycle and reduce waste to minimize landfill contributions.
  • Support renewable energy sources such as solar and wind power.
